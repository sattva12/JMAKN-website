What we do

Six services.
One outcome.

Every engagement ends the same way: your facility runs better than it did before we arrived.

01
Operational Audit
We analyze your METRC data, labor logs, and plant movement. We show you exactly where you're losing money — before we charge to fix it. This is our entry point and our proof of value.
02
Process Engineering
SOPs, cross-training schedules, harvest workflows, tag-to-table optimization. Turning tribal knowledge into a repeatable, engineered system that survives staff turnover.
03
Genetics Reset
Wild-bred genetics designed for uniform height, resilience, and minimal touch. The right plant requires a fraction of the labor of the wrong one. Via Northern Sōl Farms.
04
Tech Integration
Scanners, taggers, custom METRC workflows. Minimize computer time. Maximize time on plants. Compliance without the paralysis that kills productivity.
05
Facility Build
When the audit finds an infrastructure bottleneck, our build arm executes the fix. One contract. One team. No finger-pointing between vendors. Design through CO under one roof.
06
Ongoing Management
For new market operators: full facility management. We build it, run it, and optimize it. You hold the license. We deliver the numbers. Available for Virginia and emerging markets.
